# Refactorer Questions Template

## Mandatory Questions

These questions MUST always be asked to ensure a deep understanding of the refactor goals:

| Category | What to clarify |
|----------|----------------|
| **Primary Pain Point** | Why are we refactoring this? (Complexity, readability, performance, tech debt?) |
| **Scope** | Which specific files, classes, or functions are we touching? Any "no-go" zones? |
| **Existing Tests** | Does the project have tests? Where are they? Can I run them? |
| **Design Patterns** | Do you have a specific pattern or architecture in mind for this refactor? |
| **Definition of Done** | What does success look like? How will we verify nothing broke? |
